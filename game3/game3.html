The boss has gone wild! Defeat him before he takes you down!

Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to move around. Overlapp your character to deal damage to the boss. But watch out for the boss's attacks, b/c they hurt back.

Currently the boss's attacks include:

  • A teleport that is basically impossible to dodge. Just pray
  • A charge from one side of the screen to the other.
  • A beam that spans the screen
  • A spam of projectiles (currently not implamented)

The player also regenerates their health every second, so if you need to hang back, fell free to. And if you feel like taking some risks, go right ahead.

To reset the game, refresh the page for hit F5 on your keyboard
