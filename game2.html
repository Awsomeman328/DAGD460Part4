Gobblet!™

A Fun Game of Strategy

RULES OF THE GAME

Equipment: The game of Gobblet consists of a 16-square playing board and 24 playing pieces called gobblets: 12 white and 12 black.

Object of the Game

Be the first player to align 4 gobblets in a row.

Quick Start

In turns, players can either:
  • Put a new gobblet on the board.
  • Move one of their gobblets already on the board to any empty space.
  • Gobble up any smaller size gobblet already on the board with a bigger one.
The first player to align 4 gobblets in a row wins.
