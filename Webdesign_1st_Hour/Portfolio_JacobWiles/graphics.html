Answers to U4-M1-L1/L33, Q7 a-b

To indicate what the site is about (topic) and to give some visual information about what the text says.

  1. It does fit the purpose, organization, and style of the page
  2. All the images are very small, meaning small loading times as well
  3. They help guide the viewers' focus to the important content of the page
  4. There are no overly bright or obnoxious images
  5. There are no graphics that hold textual information that isn't already mentioned in the text it's corisponded with

This site does well on all of these guidelines and doesn't fail to meet any of them.
